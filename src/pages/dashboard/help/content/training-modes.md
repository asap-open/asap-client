# Training Modes

[Back to Home](/dashboard/help)

ASAP evaluates your workout balance based on your selected goal. You can change your training mode on the Progress page.

## Body (Bodybuilding & Aesthetics)
This mode is designed for users prioritizing muscle growth, symmetry, and visual aesthetics.
- **Target Frequency:** 4 sessions per week.
- **Muscle Focus:** Heavily biased towards the upper body "mirror muscles."
- **Expected Split:** 35% Push, 25% Pull, 25% Legs, and 15% Core.

## Strength (Powerlifting & Athleticism)
This mode is designed for athletes focusing on maximal force production in the primary compound lifts (Squat, Bench, Deadlift, Overhead Press).
- **Target Frequency:** 5 sessions per week.
- **Muscle Focus:** An even, perfectly balanced split to ensure no weak links in the kinetic chain.
- **Expected Split:** 30% Push, 30% Pull, 30% Legs, and 10% Core.

## Balanced (General Fitness)
This mode provides the exact same robust balance as the Strength mode but is evaluated on a more manageable weekly schedule for general fitness enthusiasts.
- **Target Frequency:** 4 sessions per week.
- **Muscle Focus:** Even split for comprehensive health and longevity.
- **Expected Split:** 30% Push, 30% Pull, 30% Legs, and 10% Core.
